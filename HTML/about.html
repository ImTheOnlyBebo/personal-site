Introduction

Hello! My name is Tanner Bibeau, a passionate and dedicated website developer. With a strong background in various dynamic and hands-on jobs, I bring a unique perspective and a robust set of skills to my web development projects. Whether I'm coding a website or hitting the slopes, I always strive for excellence and innovation.

Previous Jobs

Throughout my career, I've had the opportunity to work in diverse roles that have shaped my work ethic and problem-solving abilities:

  • Snowboard Instructor: Taught individuals of all ages the fundamentals and advanced techniques of snowboarding, fostering a love for the sport and ensuring safety on the slopes.

  • Ski Lift Builder: Participated in constructing ski lifts, contributing to the seamless and enjoyable experience of winter sports enthusiasts. This role required technical skills, attention to detail, and a strong understanding of engineering principles.
